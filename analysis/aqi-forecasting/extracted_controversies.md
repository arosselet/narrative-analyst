# Extracted Controversies & Dynamics: AQI Forecasting

## Core Structural Divergence
The tension lies in the shift from **Diagnostic** (Reactive) monitoring to **Proactive** (Predictive) forecasting.

| Dimension | Standard Monitoring | AQI Forecasting Model |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Philosophy** | Diagnostic | Proactive |
| **Action** | Post-exposure | Preemptive |
| **Data Focus** | Reactive Sensors (Pollutants) | Leading Indicators (Stagnation, Mobility) |
| **Goal** | Reporting / Statistics | Preemptive management (Traffic diversion) |

## The Structural Machine
-   **The Trap**: Current environmental monitoring acts as a diagnostic system. It reports pollution levels *after* citizens have been exposed.
-   **The Mechanism**: The proposal shifts the model to predict AQI 24 hours in advance by analyzing **Atmospheric Stagnation** (high pressure, low wind) and **Urban Mobility** (traffic, industrial density).
-   **The Constraint**: The model strictly **excludes individual pollutant concentrations** to force the algorithm to identify *causal drivers* (the structural machine generating the pollution) rather than simple temporal persistence (yesterday was polluted, so today is polluted).
